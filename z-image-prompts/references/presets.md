# Visual Presets Reference

Tested phrases for Z-Image, Flux, and Nano-Banana models.

## Shots

| Key | Description |
|-----|-------------|
| extreme_wide | extreme wide shot, vast environment dwarfing subject |
| wide | wide shot, full scene visible, establishing context |
| medium_wide | medium wide shot, full body visible with environment |
| medium | medium shot, waist up, conversational distance |
| medium_close | medium close-up, chest and head, intimate but not intrusive |
| close | close-up, head and shoulders, emotional focus |
| extreme_close | extreme close-up, face filling frame, intense detail |
| detail | detail shot, specific object or body part in sharp focus |

## Angles

| Key | Description |
|-----|-------------|
| eye | shot at eye level for a neutral, direct, human perspective |
| low | shot from a low angle looking upward, making subject appear dominant and towering |
| high | shot from a high angle looking down, minimizing subject stature |
| overhead | bird's-eye view, straight down from directly overhead |
| dutch | dutch angle, horizon line tilted diagonally to create tension |
| worms_eye | worm's eye view, extreme low angle from ground |

## Lighting

### Natural
| Key | Description |
|-----|-------------|
| direct_sun | direct unfiltered sunlight, strong highlights and defined shadows, high contrast |
| soft_daylight | soft diffuse daylight, even neutral illumination, minimal shadows |
| golden_hour | warm golden hour sunlight, low-angle rays, long soft shadows, amber tones |
| blue_hour | cool, soft blue hour ambient light, low contrast, serene twilight tones |
| moonlight | pale cool moonlight, gentle low-intensity highlights, subtle deep shadows |
| bioluminescent | lit by alien, organic bioluminescent glow from environment |

### Artificial
| Key | Description |
|-----|-------------|
| neon | illuminated by vibrant, saturated neon signage in pinks and cyans |
| strobe | high-output studio strobe-style illumination, controlled bursts, crisp shadow definition |
| fluorescent | lit by harsh, greenish-white overhead fluorescent tube lighting |
| candlelight | lit solely by the warm, flickering orange glow of nearby candles |
| streetlight | illuminated by amber sodium-vapor glow of urban streetlights |
| ring_light | lit by front-facing ring light, characteristic halo reflection in eyes |
| screen_glow | lit by cold, bluish digital glow of computer screen or smartphone |
| spotlight | illuminated with tight spot beam for localized high-intensity highlights |

### Mixed
| Key | Description |
|-----|-------------|
| window_tungsten | complex blend of cool outdoor window light mixed with warm indoor tungsten lamps |
| window_lamp | soft window daylight balanced with warm interior lamp for complementary tones |
| practical_rim | practical ambient sources in foreground with cool rim light separating subject from background |

## Poses

### Leg Positions
- legs_extended: sitting with both legs extended straight forward
- legs_folded_side: sitting with knees bent, both legs folded to one side
- legs_crossed: sitting with legs crossed at the shins
- stance_wide: standing with feet far apart, knees slightly turned outward
- split: legs stretched wide to both sides, hips lowered
- kneeling: knees on ground, feet tucked beneath
- on_tiptoes: heels lifted, weight on toes
- legs_dangling: sitting on edge with legs hanging freely

### Hand Gestures
- peace_sign: index and middle fingers raised in V gesture
- heart_hands: both hands forming small heart shape
- thumbs_up: thumb extended upward
- ok_sign: thumb and index forming circle, other fingers extended
- pointing: index finger extended pointing
- namaste: palms pressed together
- hands_on_hips: both hands resting on hips, elbows outward
- hands_in_pockets: hands tucked into pockets, shoulders relaxed
- shh_finger: index finger placed against lips

### Orientations
- frontal: full frontal view, torso and shoulders square to viewer
- side_profile: strict side profile, body rotated 90 degrees
- three_quarter_front: torso rotated ~45° toward viewer
- three_quarter_back: torso rotated ~45° away, back dominant
- facing_away: back fully visible, shoulders squared away

### Combined
- standing_relaxed: weight on one leg, relaxed shoulders
- leaning_wall: leaning against wall, arms crossed, one foot up
- walking: mid-stride, natural arm swing
- running: one foot off ground, arms pumping
- crouching: crouched low, weight on balls of feet
- lying_back: on back, arms at sides or behind head

## Expressions

### Gaze
- gaze_direct: eyes looking directly at viewer
- gaze_away: eyes looking off-frame
- gaze_over_shoulder: body facing away, head turned back
- gaze_upward: chin raised, eyes directed upward
- gaze_downcast: eyes cast down, softened gaze
- eyes_closed: gently closed, relaxed eyelids

### Happy
- gentle_smile: soft smile conveying warmth
- big_grin: wide toothy grin
- laughing: genuine laughter, eyes crinkled
- smirk: playful asymmetrical smirk

### Sad
- crying: tears streaming, contorted face
- melancholic: distant gaze, drooping eyelids
- disappointed: slight frown, averted eyes

### Intense
- scowling: furrowed brows, piercing glare
- determined: set jaw, steely eyes
- focused: laser-focused stare

### Subtle
- neutral: relaxed features, calm gaze
- curious: wide alert eyes
- thoughtful: looking away, hand on chin
- serene: perfect calm, eyes closed or soft
